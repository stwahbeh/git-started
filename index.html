The Lion King


Simba


Simba is the main character in the movie The Lion King. He is a prideful Lion that grows up through the death of his father. While being courageous and kind he is also silly and "Laughs in the face of danger".

Simba appears in The Lion King . The Lion King is a 90 minute movie, that tells the story of Simba as he grows from a cub to an adult. Simba's life isn't easy and takes a tragic turn when his Uncle Scar, betrays his brother and kills him in a wild-a-beast stampede.
